Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our treatments, clinic procedures, and dental health.

General Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID proof, any previous dental records or X-rays, and a list of your current medications and medical history.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend a professional cleaning and check-up every 6 months to prevent major dental issues and maintain oral hygiene.

Treatments (Braces & RCT)

Is Root Canal Treatment (RCT) painful?

With modern anesthesia and rotary endodontics, RCT is virtually painless. It is actually performed to STOP the pain caused by infection.

What is the right age for braces?

While 10-14 is ideal, orthodontic treatment can be successful at any age. We offer solutions for both children and adults (including clear aligners).

How long does a typical braces treatment take?

Most cases take between 12 to 24 months, depending on the complexity of alignment and the patient's biological response.

Emergency Care

What should I do if my tooth gets knocked out?

Keep the tooth in a container of milk and visit us within 30-60 minutes. Do not scrub the tooth root; just rinse it gently with water.

Do you handle same-day dental emergencies?

Yes, we prioritize patients in acute pain. Please call us at +91 89695 66559 as soon as possible for emergency assistance.
